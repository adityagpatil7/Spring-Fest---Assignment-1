Spring Fest, known as SF is the annual social and cultural festival of the Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur held during January in the spring semester.During the fest, students from IIT Kharagpur and from different colleges all over the country participate in a multitude of social and cultural activities.The ambit of the events encompasses various genres like Dance, Music, Dramatics, Photography, Literature, Quizzing, Food Fest and Fine Arts.
